The design trade-off

Moving from Hosted to Embedded to Custom gives progressively more design control, but also requires more engineering work. Hosted and Embedded Checkout are maintained by Stripe — they handle accessibility, localization, payment method updates, and compliance automatically. With Custom forms, your team owns all of that.
